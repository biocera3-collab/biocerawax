Stripless Brazilian Hard Wax

Our signature 100% natural hard wax provides quick, high-performance results with a less painful waxing experience. Biocera stripless wax can be applied in multiple, large sections without breaking or becoming rigid.

Heated at low temperatures to minimize irritation, it adheres only to the hair — not the skin itself. Our secret blend of natural ingredients gently and effectively removes both fine and coarse hair with one application, leaving the skin smooth and conditioned by beeswax and essential oils.
