Quick tips · From the chair

Small adjustments, big results.

Tip / 01

Warm, never hot.

If you can't hold a drop on the inside of your wrist, it's too hot. Biocera is designed to work at a lower temperature than you're used to.

Tip / 02

One pass, same area — no irritation.

Because our wax adheres only to the hair, you can pass over the same area multiple times without stripping skin.

Tip / 03

Let the wax set. Then flick.

A patient set, a quick flick — against the direction of growth, parallel to the skin. That's the whole technique.

Tip / 04

Oil immediately. Not five minutes later.

Apply Bee Smooth Oil the moment the last strip is pulled. The pores are open. That's the moment the oil does its work.
