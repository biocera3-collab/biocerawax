What your skin actually absorbs.

Hair removal is not a rinse-off category. Even trace solvents, fragrance carriers, and cheap plastics in a depilatory base can spend meaningful time against barrier-disrupted skin.

That is why Biocera keeps the ingredient list short: beeswax as structure, botanicals for glide and finish, and no artificial color or fragrance to hide behind.

For professionals, the practical takeaway is simple: read the wax label like you read a serum label. If a client asks what is in the pot, you should be proud of every line.
