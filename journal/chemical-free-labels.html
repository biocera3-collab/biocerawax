What "chemical free" actually means.

Everything is chemistry, including water. What clients mean when they say chemical free is usually: no mystery solvents, no plastic-smelling resins, no dyes to hide oxidation.

Honest labeling should separate what is in the pot from what is marketing. If an ingredient hides behind a number or a trademark blend, ask why.

Biocera publishes the story the way we would want it read in a treatment room: beeswax forward, botanicals named, and temperature guidance that matches how the product behaves.
