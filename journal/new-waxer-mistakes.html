The Craft · 5 min read

Three mistakes most new waxers make.

Overheating is the first mistake because it feels faster. It is not — it creates hesitation, second passes, and unhappy skin.

The second mistake is laying strips like frosting. Stripless film works in disciplined ribbons; thickness only slows set and dulls the pull.

The third mistake is pulling up instead of parallel to the body. Biocera rewards a confident, low, parallel flick that respects hair direction.
