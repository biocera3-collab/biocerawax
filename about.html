Founder's Note

Rosana Michail. A salon, a mother, a formula.

Rosana grew up in São Paulo, Brazil, where her mother owned a large salon and formulated her own natural depilatory wax. Rosana was trained by her mother in waxing — then went on to study chemistry, to take the family formulas to the next level.

Once in the United States, Rosana quickly realized there was a huge opportunity to give clients a better — 100% natural — waxing experience than the one they'd been used to in North America.

After getting her esthetician license, she opened her first waxing spa in Manhattan. Over ten years, dozens of salons and professional waxers asked to buy her wax. She always said no; she only made enough for her own rooms.

Now, Biocera is how we bring that 40-year-old formula to every serious waxer, everywhere.

Chapter 04 — The Formula

A stripless Brazilian wax, perfected over four decades.

Chemical free, plant based, with no artificial colors or fragrance. Paraben free. Gluten free. Made only with the highest quality natural ingredients — including organic essential oils. The wax goes on warm, not hot, eliminating the chance of burning sensitive skin and opening the hair root for easier removal.

01 — Beeswax
Conditions the skin

Leaves the skin smooth and nourished as the wax is pulled away — a finishing step, built in.

02 — Organic essential oils
Calm and scent

A natural, honeyed aroma — never the harsh chemical smell of synthetic wax.

03 — Plant-based resins
Grip only the hair

Adheres to hair, not to the top layer of skin, so it can be applied multiple times without irritation.

04 — What's not in it
Zero compromises

No parabens, no gluten, no artificial color, no artificial fragrance. Nothing loaded with cheap chemistry.
