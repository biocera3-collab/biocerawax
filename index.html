02 — What we believe

Seven beliefs that make a formula.

01
Belief 01 / 07Clean beauty

We believe in clean, natural beauty products, sourced responsibly.

Whatever you put on the skin is partially absorbed by the body. So we treat every ingredient like it matters — because it does.

Belief 02 / 07Craft

We believe in selecting the finest ingredients — then using them creatively.

High performance doesn't come from more chemistry. It comes from better choices, made patiently, over generations.

Belief 03 / 07Industry

Too many wax lines push gadgets and add-ons instead of the wax itself.

We keep the spotlight on one stripless blend — huge crates, honest ingredients, shipped from New Jersey.

Belief 04 / 07Community

We believe in independent salons and spas.

And we support the passions and dreams of every waxer working in one.

Belief 05 / 07Skin

Anything on the skin should be non-toxic, hypoallergenic, conditioning.

That's not a product claim. It's a starting condition.

Belief 06 / 07Joy

We believe in having fun, being passionate, bringing confident skin to all.

Waxing is intimate work. It deserves a product that respects the moment.

Belief 07 / 07Formula

Warm, never hot. On the hair — never on the skin.

Our formula goes on warm, adheres only to the hair, and leaves skin smooth and conditioned by beeswax and natural oils.
